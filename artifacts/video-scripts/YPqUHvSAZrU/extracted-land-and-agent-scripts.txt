## Agent page scripts

### Resale price reality check
- Scope: agents
- Channel: Call
- Source time: 16:21-16:58
- Use when: a land Realtor is defending a high listed/agent-presented lot price and we need local resale truth without insulting the seller.

Script:
I am looking for an agent who can help me buy land in {{market}} and then sell it again cleanly. If you were helping me buy this lot and then relist it, what price would I need to buy it at so you could realistically sell it again?

I am open to paying a strong land commission if you can help me move it, but I need the buy price to make sense for the current market.

### Fair price check
- Scope: agents
- Channel: Call
- Source time: 16:00-16:28
- Use when: an agent is defending a land listing price and we need market-based pushback without sounding combative.

Script:
Do you think that is a fair price for this market right now?

If I bought it there, what would you expect we could realistically resell it for, and how long do you think that would take? I am not trying to beat anyone up; I just need the project math to be real.

### Would seller entertain it?
- Scope: agents
- Channel: Call / Email
- Source time: 10:44-11:22
- Use when: internal comping produces a specific land offer and the property is listed or agent-controlled.

Script:
I may be off, and I do not want to waste your time. Based on the land comps and what I would need to make the project work, I would probably be around {{offerAmount}}.

Do you think the seller would entertain something in that neighborhood, or is that too far away to even put in front of them?

## Land page scripts

### Why not retail?
- Scope: deals
- Channel: Call / SMS
- Source time: 07:30-07:57 / 15:58-16:08
- Use when: an owner says the lot is worth retail or gives a high number, before arguing comps.

Script:
It sounds like a nice lot. Help me understand: why not list it with a Realtor and try for the highest retail price?

If speed, privacy, or a simpler off-market sale matters more, I can look at it through that lens. If top retail is the main goal, listing may honestly be the better path.

### Other lots + hidden facts
- Scope: deals
- Channel: Call / SMS
- Source time: 14:12-14:30
- Use when: near the end of land-owner discovery before pricing or follow-up.

Script:
Is it currently listed with a Realtor anywhere? If it is, I want to handle it the right way.

Do you have any other lots you are thinking about selling? And is there anything about this property I should know before I price it: access, utilities, flood, wetlands, HOA, liens, title, or anything unusual?
