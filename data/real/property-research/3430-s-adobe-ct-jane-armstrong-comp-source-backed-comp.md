## 7. Market read

Pahrump has a very large supply of sub-acre vacant lots. LandWatch alone shows hundreds of active sub-acre listings under $50k. That creates weak urgency for an end buyer unless the parcel has a specific advantage: utility access, paved road, water/sewer nearby, better zoning, corner lot, or in-fill adjacency.

For this parcel:

Positive:

- Real county-verified vacant residential lot.
- VR-10 zoning.
- Taxes appear paid/current.
- Pahrump has ongoing custom-home and manufactured-home buyer demand.
- 0.30 acre is more usable than very tiny 0.12–0.16 acre lots.

Negative:

- Huge active supply below $35k.
- Many larger lots are asking less than $35k.
- County assessed value is very low relative to asking price.
- Current ownership/name stack includes Jane plus Byers names; title/signing authority must be clarified.
- Need to verify utilities, water/sewer/service line distance, flood/wash, road surface, and buildability.
