If we assume rough retail/ARV scenarios:

- **Low package retail:** $350k
- **Mid package retail:** $400k–$425k
- **High package retail:** $450k+ if the home is clean, larger, and the extra fenced lot is attractive

Investor rough math before repairs:

- 70% of $350k = $245k
- 70% of $400k = $280k
- 70% of $425k = $297.5k
- 70% of $450k = $315k

Repairs, closing costs, holding costs, and assignment spread come out of those numbers. That means a real cash/investor offer may easily need to be in the **mid/high-$200ks to low-$300ks**, depending on condition — not enough to excite a retail-minded owner unless they need certainty.
